#############
  Locations
#############

There are two principle earldoms struggling with each other, and with the bandit army that plagues their border.
In this chapter, we'll take a close look at Hillshire and Southlands and some important locations in each earldom.

For some background on the Kingdom of the East, see :ref:`introduction.geography`. Southlands has a useful deep-water port. Hillshire has a port city, but the bay is small and open to the ocean, limiting commerce to small ships.

In this chapter, we'll look at *all* of the locations mentioned in the various scenarios.
There are a lot, they're organized into three areas:

-   In cities of Hillshire, generally City of Eagles.

-   In the wilderness of Hillshire.

-   In the cities of Southlands, general the City of White Water.
