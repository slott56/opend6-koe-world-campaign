7. Flush out the Jackal¶

Location:

Somewhere in Hillshire, The Jackal’s Secret Cave.

Date:

Late summer

Context¶

The party has recovered Sir Bond’s Lightning Rod. Ideally, they’ve returned it to Sir Warrant. Perhaps not, perhaps they have other plans.

Hoping to salvage something, Xorn has fled into the mountains to a fastness in an old Goblin cave.

Complication¶

The party needs to find Xorn and bring him to justice. Or kill him while trying to bring up to Earl Hillshire.

Crisis¶

It’s a cave full of goblins (and worse).

See The Jackal’s Secret Cave.

Climax¶

Xorn is only a servant to the Salamander.

The Salamander is difficult to defeat, but will retreat to it’s fire realm when three things are true:

  1. Xorn is dead, and

  2. it takes injury, and

  3. it finds it can’t control any of the party members.

Consequences¶

This is worth Funds gifts from both knights, and Fate Point rewards.

The Bandit King
