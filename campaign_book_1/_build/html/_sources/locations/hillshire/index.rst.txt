#############
  Hillshire
#############

The Earldom of Hillshire is the south-western corner of the Kingdom of the East.
This is (generally) where the characters will start.

The **Keep of the Cult of the Jackal** is also in Hillshire. It's covered in a separate chapter, :ref:`location.keep`.
The Bandit army is there, which means bandits tend to prey on caravans in Hillshire.

To the southeast of Hillshire lies the earldom of Southlands. Once a caravan comes down out of the hills, it's relatively safe, and can proceed to the City of White Water without further problems from bandits.

There isn't a big city which serves as the center of Power in Hillshire.
This is a new addition to the kingdom, and it lacks a well-established network of wealthy cities.
A once-powerful mining operation was centered on a single castle and some surrounding villages.

..  admonition:: GM Note

    Wandering around the roads in the wilderness of Hillshire can expose the party to the Bandit Army and Goblin Hordes.
    See :ref:`location.wilderness` for a table to generate random wilderness encounters when characters don't have a skilled guide.
