Wilderness of Hillshire¶

Travel in the corner of Hillshire bordering on Southlands can be challenging because of the Bandit Army and Goblin hordes. During the day, the Bandit Army will pursue caravans. During the night, the Goblin Hordes will harass anyone they find: a camp fire draws goblins.

The base for the Bandit Army (and the Goblin Hordes) is a day or so north of City of Eagles. This makes for dangerous travel outside the city walls.

Any travel in Hillshire outside the city walls runs a risk of encounters with unfriendly forces. See Encounters for details.
