Southlands¶

The Earldom of Southlands is the southernmost coastline of the Kingdom of the East. These adventures are centered on the City of White Water.

To the north-east of Southlands lies Hillshire. The caravan guards worry about crossing that border, and entering the hill country where bandits seem to operate freely.

As background for the GM, the center of power in Southlands is the coastal City of Grace. It’s unlikely the party will need to visit this trading hub and human cesspit.
