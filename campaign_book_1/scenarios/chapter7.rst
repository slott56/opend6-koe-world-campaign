Flush out the Jackal
==============================

:Location: Somewhere in Hillshire, :ref:`location.cave`.

:Date: Late summer

..  structure: context complication crisis climax (i.e., battle) consequences.

Context
-------

The party has recovered Sir Bond's **Lightning Rod**.
Ideally, they've returned it to Sir Warrant.
Perhaps not, perhaps they have other plans.

Hoping to salvage something, Xorn has fled into the mountains to a fastness in an old Goblin cave.


Complication
-------------

The party needs to find Xorn and bring him to justice.
Or kill him while trying to bring up to Earl Hillshire.

Crisis
------

It's a cave full of goblins (and worse).

See :ref:`location.cave`.

Climax
------

Xorn is only a servant to the Salamander.

The Salamander is difficult to defeat, but will retreat to it's fire realm when three things are true:

a. Xorn is dead, and
b. it takes injury, and
c. it finds it can't control any of the party members.

Consequences
------------

This is worth Funds gifts from both knights, and Fate Point rewards.
