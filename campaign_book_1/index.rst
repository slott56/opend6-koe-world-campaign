OpenD6 Fantasy Campaign: The Bandit King
=============================================

This is an OpenD6 Fantasy Campaign designed for the Kingdom of the East.
It provides details of places, people, and situations set in the Kingdom of the East.
This material presumes you're familiar with the :external:ref:`OpenD6 Fantasy Rulebook<fantasy_rulebook>` rules and have the :external:ref:`OpenD6 Fantasy Worldbook: Kingdom of The East<world.world_book>`.

..  important:: No Campaign Survives Contact with Players

    This campaign description is intentionally open-ended.
    There are no mandatory encounters or locations.
    The sequence of events is flexible, and deviations are possible.
    This material is a seed, from which the GM and the players will grow their own unique story.

For more information on the world, see :external:ref:`world.world_book`.

This Campaign Book provides a brief introduction to Kingdom of the East.
It focuses on the principle actors: two knights trying to sort out their problems, and an agent of chaos making things worse for everybody.
The book provides seven scenarios of varying complexity.
Two of the scenarios may involve multiple sessions of play, depending on the characters involved.
Finally, this book includes 46 maps of locations in which the action takes place.
