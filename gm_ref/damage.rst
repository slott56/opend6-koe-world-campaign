.. _`damage`:

Damage & Healing
================

Damage Magnitude
----------------

**Damage Dice to Roll** =

-   Weapon's D (If leading "+", add Strength Damage)
-   \+ combat action effects
-   \+ damage bonus of Result Points + 5.

Strength Damage = Physique or *lifting* skill + 2

**Defender's Damage Resistance Dice to Roll** = Armor, Special Abilities.
Reduced for disease, poisons, Hindrances, etc.
(May be zero, which means no roll and no resistance.)

Fate Point lets defender use Physique roll for damage resistance.

**1. Roll Damage Resistance Dice** (if any).

Character Point can buy 1D more damage resistance.

**2. Roll Damage Dice**.

Character Point can buy 1D more damage resistance.

**3. Result**.

    -   Damage Dice ≤ Damage Resistance Dice. No change.

    -   Damage Dice > Damage Resistance Dice: deduct result points (damage - resistance) from Body.

**Special Cases**

:Stun Only:
    Subtract Physique or *stamina* skill roll from damage total.
    What's left is minutes of unconsciousness.

Healing
-------

Once per day.

Full day of rest without *healing* skill. Roll Physique ± modifier.

-   Modifier.

    :Full rest: +1D
    :light activity:
    :running and fighting: -1D

With *healing* skill. Make *healing* skill roll and roll magnitude of result.

..  csv-table:: Healing Result Points
    :header: Magnitude Roll, Body Recovered

    0, 0
    1-5, 2
    6-10, 1D
    11-15, 2D
    16-20, 3D
    roll, (roll ÷ 5) - 1 D
