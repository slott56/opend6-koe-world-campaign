Character Basics¶

Attributes¶

Distribute 18D; most are range 1D to 5D. Extranormal can be 0D.

1D = 3 pips; +1 is ⅓D, +2 is ⅔D.

Agility:

Balance, limberness, quickness, and full-body motor abilities.

Coordination:

Hand-eye coordination and fine motor abilities.

Physique:

Physical power, and resistance to damage.

Charisma:

Emotional strength, physical attractiveness, and personality.

Intellect:

Memory and ability to learn.

Acumen:

Mental quickness, creativity, and attention to detail

Extranormal:

Magic: alteration, apportation, divination, conjuration

Miracles: divination, favor, strife

See character sheet.
