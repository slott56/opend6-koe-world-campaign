Damage & Healing¶

Damage Magnitude¶

Damage Dice to Roll =

  • Weapon’s D (If leading “+”, add Strength Damage)

  • + combat action effects

  • + damage bonus of Result Points + 5.

Strength Damage = Physique or lifting skill + 2

Defender’s Damage Resistance Dice to Roll = Armor, Special Abilities. Reduced for disease, poisons, Hindrances, etc. (May be zero, which means no roll and no resistance.)

Fate Point lets defender use Physique roll for damage resistance.

1. Roll Damage Resistance Dice (if any).

Character Point can buy 1D more damage resistance.

2. Roll Damage Dice.

Character Point can buy 1D more damage resistance.

3. Result.

  • Damage Dice ≤ Damage Resistance Dice. No change.

  • Damage Dice > Damage Resistance Dice: deduct result points (damage - resistance) from Body.

Special Cases

Stun Only:

Subtract Physique or stamina skill roll from damage total. What’s left is minutes of unconsciousness.
