Combat¶

1. Initiative¶

Initiative order:

  1. ability or talent that allows the character to go first (e.g, Surprise)

  2. Perception/Acumen attribute (survival or awareness skills)

  3. search skill

  4. Agility attribute

  5. dodge skill

  6. Equipment or situation that allows the character to go before another character.

Quick Draw requires doing initiative rolls using Acumen. Character can shift dice from weapon skill to Acumen.

Surprise. Either gain initiative OR +1D on first action.

2c. Roll Dice¶

If Passive Defense:

  • No roll: base 10 ± action ± skill ± modifiers.

If Non-Passive Defense:

  • Roll Defensive Skill, adjusted by action ± skill ± modifiers.

Roll Attack Dice.

Outcome:

  • Attacker roll ≥ Combat difficulty: a hit.

  • Attacker roll ≫ Combat difficulty: damage bonus = Result Points + 5.

3. Damage¶

Damage Dice to Roll =

  • Weapon’s D (If leading “+”, add Strength Damage)

  • + combat action effects

  • + damage bonus of Result Points + 5.

Strength Damage = Physique or lifting skill + 2

Defender’s Damage Resistance Dice to Roll = Armor, Special Abilities. Reduced for disease, poisons, Hindrances, etc. (May be zero, which means no roll and no resistance.)

See Damage & Healing.

Actions¶

Multi-action Penalty: -1D for every full action above the character’s slots (default is 1). Note: “Wait and React” is two actions; waiting doesn’t have a difficulty, the reaction will have a -1D penalty.
