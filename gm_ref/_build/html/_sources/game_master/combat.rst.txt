Combat
=======

1. Initiative
--------------

Initiative order:

(1) ability or talent that allows the character to go first (e.g, Surprise)
(2) Perception/Acumen attribute (*survival* or *awareness* skills)
(3) *search* skill
(4) Agility attribute
(5) *dodge* skill
(6) Equipment or situation that allows the character to go before another character.

**Quick Draw** requires doing initiative rolls using Acumen.
Character can shift dice from weapon skill to Acumen.

**Surprise**. Either gain initiative OR +1D on first action.

2. Attack and Defend
----------------------

2a.  Offense Dice
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

**Offense Dice to Roll** = Attribute + Skills + advantage dice - limitation dice

2b.  Defense Difficulty
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

**Defense Modes**

:passive:
    10

:active:
    (After defender's turn in current round)

    defensive skill (*dodge*, *block*, *parry*, etc.) roll

:full:
    (No other action)

    defensive skill roll + 10

:partial:
    (After defender's turn in current round, multi-action penalty)

    defensive skill roll

2c. Roll Dice
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If Passive Defense:

- No roll: base 10 ± action ± skill ± modifiers.

If Non-Passive Defense:

-   **Roll Defensive Skill**, adjusted by action ± skill ± modifiers.

**Roll Attack Dice**.

Outcome:

-   Attacker roll ≥ Combat difficulty: a hit.
-   Attacker roll ≫ Combat difficulty: damage bonus = Result Points + 5.

3. Damage
----------

**Damage Dice to Roll** =

-   Weapon's D (If leading "+", add Strength Damage)
-   \+ combat action effects
-   \+ damage bonus of Result Points + 5.

Strength Damage = Physique or *lifting* skill + 2

**Defender's Damage Resistance Dice to Roll** = Armor, Special Abilities.
Reduced for disease, poisons, Hindrances, etc.
(May be zero, which means no roll and no resistance.)

See :ref:`Damage`.

Actions
--------

Multi-action Penalty: -1D for every full action above the character's slots (default is 1).
Note: "Wait and React" is two actions; waiting doesn't have a difficulty, the reaction will have a -1D penalty.

Knockout, Stun-Only
--------------------

Called shot to head (10-50cm) +12 difficulty.

- >=50% of body, uncounscious for hours = result points
    does 1/2 to body

- < 50% of body, takes ordinary body damage
