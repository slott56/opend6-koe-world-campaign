Character Options
=================

Advantages
----------

Skill Die Cost 1D = 1 Rank

-   Authority (Local R1, State R2, National R3)

-   Contacts (Minor R1, Major R2, Epic R3, Mythic R4)

-   Cultures (Tourist R1, Immigrant R2, Native R3, Cope with Aliens R4)

-   Equipment (Specialized R1, Prohibited R2, Heroic R3, Weird, R4)

-   Fame (Local Diff=15 R1, National Diff=15/Local Diff=8 R2, Universal Diff=8 R3)

-   Patrons (One Expedition R1, Some Expeditions R2, All Expeditions R3) Patrons can bring in lower-rank Enemies, also.

-   Size (+3 scale each rank)

-   Trademark Specialization

-   Wealth (Gets By R1, Comfortable R2, Well Off R3)


Disadvantages
--------------

Skill Die Gain 1D = 1 Rank

-   Achilles' Heel (Major R3, Severe R4)

    -   Allergy 3D damage
    -   Atmospheric Incompatibility +1 difficulty,
    -   Cultural Allergy,
    -   Environmental Incompatibility +1 difficulty,
    -   Metabolic Difference damage resistance -1,
    -   Nutritional Requirements,
    -   Rot,
    -   Vulnerability opponent +5 attack

-   Advantage Flaw (Minor R1, Constant R2, Secondary Disadvantage R3)

    -   Infect Others,
    -   Overcome Stigma,
    -   Stench

-   Age (Teen R1, Child R2)

-   Bad Luck (Rare R2, Infrequent R3, Awkward R4)

-   Burn-out (R1, ...)

-   Cultural Unfamiliarity (Outsider R1, Struggling R2, Alien R3)

-   Debt (Small R1, Significant R2, Everything R3)

-   Devotion (Private R1, Public R2, Zealot R3)

-   Employed (Job R1, Constraining Job R2, Enslaved R3)

-   Enemy (Rare R1, Frequent R2, Powerful R3)

-   Hindrance (R1, ...)

    -   Bad Knee,
    -   Rude,
    -   Trick Shoulder,
    -   Uncoordinated,
    -   Unobservant

-   Illiterate

-   Infamy [See *Fame*, but bad]

-   Language Problems (Struggling R2)

-   Learning Problems

-   Poverty

-   Prejudice (Minority R1, Oppressed Minority R2)

-   Price to Use (Infrequent R1, Frequent R2)

-   Quirk (Minor R1, Major R2, Debilitating R3)

    -   Dependency,
    -   Kleptomania,
    -   Indecision,
    -   Stutter,
    -   Secret,
    -   Paranoid,
    -   Phobic,
    -   Vengeful

-   Reduced Attribute (R2), a -1 adjustment to rolls

-    Wild Luck
