Remote Cache
~~~~~~~~~~~~

:Skill: Apportation
:Difficulty: 16 
:Effect: 38 (Possession and Apportation: Apportation to another realm (R3); Limited to 50 kg)
:Range: 1 m \(0)
:Speed:  \(0)
:Duration: 5 min \(12)
:Casting Time: 5 sec \(4)

:Other Aspects:

    incantation (4): Veil Opening Chant (litany)


    concentration (2): Concentration: 5 sec (willpower/mettle roll 8)


    components (3): Fixed location (e.g. cupboard) (common)





:Other Conditions:
    (0): Controller: None


    (3): Multiple previous visits


    (3): Small chance of loss




The mage can reach through a small opening into a known space in another realm to keep things. The other realm location is not 100% safe; a critical failure may mean the cache was moved in the other realm. The opening is centered on a specific architectural feature, like a cupboard in the mage's study.
