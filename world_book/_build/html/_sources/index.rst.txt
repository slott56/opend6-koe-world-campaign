OpenD6 Fantasy Worldbook: Kingdom of the East
=============================================

This is an OpenD6 "Worldbook" -- it describes an OpenD6 Fantasy setting and provides some new rules.
This material presumes you're familiar with the :external:ref:`OpenD6 Fantasy Rulebook <fantasy_rulebook>`.
This book provides extensions to the rules and some useful background information common to all campaigns and adventures.

The documents are organized with an eye toward playability.
This means that background information is generally supplied *after* the essential OpenD6 rules.

This background information includes some elements of anthropology, economics, business (commerce, finance, etc.), education,
history, law, languages and literature. The focus is on geography,  politics and governance, religion, technology, and magic.

Consistent with the *OpenD6* rulebooks, what follows is a *Game System Summary* for
this Worldbook.

..  admonition:: OpenD6 Fantasy, "Kingdom of the East"

   :Game Designer:
      S. Lott

   :Genre:
      Fantasy

   :World Overview:
      Internal struggles for control over the kingdom.
      External threats include pirates from the north, the outlanders from the west (and an empire further west.)
      From the south, there are unscrupulous traders looking to acquire land.

   :Technology Level:
      Late bronze age, early iron age. Outlanders are paleolithic.

      The wheel is notably absent from **all** technology.

   **Powers**

   :Types:
      Magic with some Miracles.

   :Limitations and Restrictions:
      Magic is free-form with few fixed enchantments.
      Magic has two parts: a link with the target (thing or person) and then an elemental alteration.
      **Alteration** and **Divination** are central.
      **Conjuration** is extremely difficult.
      **Apportation** involves hidden complications.
      **Psychic** is an added area.

   :Power Skill Names:
      None.

   **Combat**

   :Damage System:
      Body Points.

      Formula: *the default*, Physique roll + 20.

   :Round Structure:
      Initiative-based.

   :Options:
      None

   **Notes**

   Elements of the *OpenD6 Fantasy Worldbook: Kingdom of the East*.

   This world book includes the following general topics:

   -  Attributes and skills. This will also have some geography, economic, and political background to put the skills into perspective.
   -  Combat, Damage, and Healing.
   -  Extranormal Skills. This includes magic and miracles. This will cover a great deal of religion, some education, and language topics to provide more depth on the magic system.
   -  Equipment. Some geography and technology information will help put this into perspective.
   -  Additional details on Magic, Enchantments, Invocations, and Artifacts.


A profound influence on these rules is [Galloway82]_.

    "A guide to fantasy wargaming with complete playing rules and sections on: Monsters & magic beings; Cultures, myth and religion; Character generation; Role playing; Spells; Warrior tables; Motivations, index and more. Book club edition, 1982, 300pp. Compiled and edited by Bruce Galloway."

.. [Galloway82]  Galloway, Bruce. *Fantasy Wargaming*, 1982.  https://archive.org/details/fantasy-wargaming-1982/
