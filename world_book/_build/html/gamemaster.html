Gamemaster Tips¶

You’ve made it this far, so you probably don’t want even more “rules.” We’ll keep this chapter short by offering a list of helpful ideas. Generally, the OpenD6 Fantasy rules can be used with some modifications. See the OpenD6 Fantasy, “Gamemaster Tips” chapter. Some further guidance for this world is appropriate.

Some players may balk at the technology limitations in this world: no wheel, no complex devices. They may suggest that it’s not “realistic” to forbid the wheel. It’s important to note that adding magic and interdimensional is not very realistic, either. As an example, the indigenous North Americans didn’t seem to embrace the wheel, either.

The core conceit behind this world is that a focus on magic removes the urge to create sophisticated technology. Encourage the players to restate any desired technologies as some kind of spell.

What technology is available is barely even bronze-age. There is limited access to iron or steel. Only the large mage temples can even attempt to forge iron or steel weapons.
