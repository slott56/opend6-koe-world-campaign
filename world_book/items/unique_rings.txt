
Ring of Pain
~~~~~~~~~~~~

A plain looking ring made of lead, incompletely transmuted to gold, with an inscription of curing a specific disease or preventing a specific wound -- the inscribed disease or wound is *actually* a trigger for additional damage from the ring.

:Effect: 18 (Damage to the wearer physical 3*D (ignores all armor))
:Other Conditions:
    (1): burn_out (R1) can be lost or stolen


    (2): Triggered by the disease or wound type in the inscription


    controller: Hælan


    Arcane Knowledge: Controller: Baelu


    Arcane Knowledge: Base Enchantment: Immediate Hurt


:Price: D (20G)

Ring of Fire
~~~~~~~~~~~~

An iron ring with a black onyx stone surrounding a sapphire, this will summon a small amount of fire from nearby sources.

:Effect: 12 (Move small fire element 4*D (physical damage))
:Range: 20 m \(7)
:Other Conditions:
    (1): burn_out (R1) can be lost or stolen


    (2): Fire element must be found somewhere nearby


    Arcane Knowledge: controller: Cyþan


    Arcane Knowledge: Base Enchantment: Light Fire


:Price: H (800G)
