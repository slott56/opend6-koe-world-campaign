
Chalice of Purity
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

More like a wide bowl than a cup, this will enhance any food eaten from it.

:Effect: 6 (Enhancements: purify 4D of impurity; mass of food that fits in the chalice 1 kg)
:Other Conditions:
    (1): burn_out (R1) can be lost or stolen


    Arcane Knowledge: controller: Hælan


    Arcane Knowledge: Base Enchantment: Enhance Food


:Price: H (300G)



Jug of Plenty
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

A pitcher, or ewer that will slowly fill with whatever liquid is in it. For water, it creates a liter in about an hour. For something more complex, like wine or beer, it will make two hours. For something very complicated, like a potion, the time depends on the base difficulty of the potion.

:Effect: 12 (Multiplication: proper balance of elements 4D; mass of liquid in jug 1 kg)
:Other Conditions:
    (1): burn_out (R1) can be lost or stolen


    Arcane Knowledge: controller: Witan


    Arcane Knowledge: Base Invocation: Multiply Food


:Price: H (600G)



Mug of Sleep
~~~~~~~~~~~~

common ceramic mug; when someone drinks from this mug, the mug will have a successful link and will attempt the R4 Sleep Enchantment.

:Effect: 12 (Narcolepsy (R4), -4D to mental and physical attributes)
:Duration: 1 hr \(18)
:Other Conditions:
    (1): burn_out (R1) can be lost or stolen


    Arcane Knowledge: controller: Hildeþrymm


    Arcane Knowledge: Base Enchantment: Sleep


:Price: H (1500G)
