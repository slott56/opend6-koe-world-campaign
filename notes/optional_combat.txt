
Optional
*********

See https://opend6project.wordpress.com/2024/08/10/melee-combat/ for additional commentary on Combat.

The core rules treat all actions as requiring one round.

More careful reading suggests an optional "Full Action" + "Bonus Action" per round.
Bonus actions take very little or no time.
A Free Action is still available be done concurrently.

Actions
--------

Multi-action Penalty: -1D for every full action above the character's slots (default is 1).
Note: "Wait and React" is two actions; waiting doesn't have a difficulty, the reaction will have a -1D penalty.

Additional actions that consume 1 round:

..  csv-table:: Optional Actions
    :header: Action, Skills

    **Attack**
    **Defend**
    **Move**
    **Force an Opponent**
    **Recover**, (new)
    , *stamina*\ : recover and *healing* self
    , *survival*\ : focus composure
    , *healing* heal other


Bonus Actions that consume less than a round:

..  csv-table:: Bonus Actions
    :header: Action, Skills

    **Gain an Advantage**
    , *bluff: misdirection*
    , *investigation* or *scholar* to find weakness
    , *survival: awareness* to increase iniative
    **Interact**, to create Composure Condition
    , *charm* to taunt or insult
    , *intimidation* to intimidate
    , *command* to demoraliza
    **Move** (under 50% of Move), "*acrobatics*"
    **Ready an Action**
    Hold a full-defense action
    Hold an action
    **Support a Character**


Gain an Advantage ranks, when roll > opposition or difficulty.

..  csv-table:: Advantage Ranks
    :header: rank, effects

    1, "+2 to a single ability check for one turn;\nAdvantage on a single ability check"
    2, "+1D to a single ability check for one round;\n+1 bonus action for one round"
    3, "+1D to a single skill for one encounter;\n+1 full action for one round"
    4, "+1D to all skills of one attribute"
    5, "+1 new ability"



Results, Conditions, and Recovery
------------------------------------

From ability check, get result points.
Result points have ranks that can add or remove conditions.

..  csv-table:: Conditions
    :header: Result Points,Rank,Condition Severity,Condition Duration

    0,0,base outcome,
    1-5,1,minimal,one full turn
    6-10,2,adequate,one full round
    11-15,3,moderate,until the end of the encounter
    16-20,4,complete,until the end of the scene
    21-25,5,excessive,until recovered
    26+,6,overwhelming,

Rank Effects. Note most are ±, depending whether they apply to ally, enemy, or self.

..  csv-table:: Rank Effects
    :header: Rank;Effect
    :delim: ;

    1;create an immediate effect roll (eg., bonus damage)
    ;± 1D: an effect roll created this turn gains ± 1D
    ;± 2: a single skill check gains ± 2 until the end of the its next turn
    ;±1WD: a single skill check gains ± 1 Wild Die until the end of its next turn
    ;Advantage: a single skill check is made with Advantage until the end of its next turn
    ;Disadvantage: a single skill check is made with Disadvantage until the end of its next turn
    ;Add/remove one full round to an existing condition.
    2;± 1D: a single skill check gains ± 1D until the end of the next round
    ;± 3: a single fixed value gains ± 3 until the end of the next round
    ;± 1 bonus action in the next round
    ;Extend an existing condition to the end of the encounter.
    ;Reduce an existing condition’s duration from the end of the encounter to 1D rounds.
    3;± 1D: a single skill gains ± 1D until the end of the encounter
    ;± 1 full action in the next round
    ;Extend an existing condition to the end of the scene.
    ;Reduce an existing condition’s duration from the end of the scene to the end of the encounter.
    4;± 1D: a single ability gains ± 1D until the end of the scene
    ;Require an existing condition to be intentionally recovered.
    ;Reduce an existing condition’s duration from requiring intentional recovery to expiring at the end of the scene.
    5;± 1 ability until the end of the scene

Varieties of Conditions and their Ranks:

..  csv-table:: Conditions
    :header:  Rank,Damage,Fatigue,Composure

    0,Normal,Normal,Normal
    1,Stunned,Breathless,Startled
    2,Hurt,Winded,Disoriented
    3,Wounded,Tired,Discouraged
    4,Incapacitated,Exhausted,Demoralized
    5,Out of Action,Out of Action,Out of Action

Damage Ranks.

..  csv-table:: Damage
    :header:  Rank,Damage,Effect

    1,Stunned,Character suffers -2 to all ability checks until the end of their next turn.
    2,Hurt,Character suffers -1D to all ability checks until the end of their next round.
    3,Wounded,Character suffers -1D to all Dexterity checks until the end of the encounter.
    4,Incapacitated,Character makes all ability checks at disadvantage.
    5,Out of Action,Character is taken out of action.

Fatigue Ranks.

..  csv-table:: Fatigue
    :header:  Rank,Fatigue,Effect

    1,Breathless,Character makes all ability checks at disadvantage until the end of their next turn.
    2,Winded,Character may not take a bonus action until the end of the next round.
    3,Tired,Character suffers -2 to their fixed ability scores until the end of the encounter.
    4,Exhausted,Character suffers -1D to all Strength checks until the end of the scene.
    5,Out of Action,Character is taken out of action.

Composure Ranks

..  csv-table:: Composure
    :header:  Rank,Composure,Effect

    1,Startled,Character suffers -2 to their initiative on the next round.
    2,Disoriented,Character makes all ability checks at disadvantage until the end of their next round.
    3,Discouraged,Character may not take a bonus action until the end the of the encounter.
    4,Demoralized,Character suffers -1D to all Presence checks until the end of the scene.
    5,Out of Action,Character is taken out of action.

Recovery ranks from result points

..  csv-table:: Recovery
    :header: rank, effects

    1,Recover a rank 1 condition; or +1D to the next recovery check
    2,Recover a rank 2 condition; or reduce an existing condition’s duration from the end of the encounter to 1D rounds
    3,Recover a rank 3 condition; or reduce an existing condition’s duration from the end of the scene to the end of the encounter
    4,Recover a rank 4 condition; or reduce an existing condition’s duration from requiring intentional recovery to expiring at the end of the scene
    5,Recover a rank 5 condition
