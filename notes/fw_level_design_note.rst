The Notion of Character Level
=============================

The original design stemmed from the Fantasy Wargaming rules. This was adapted to the Hero system.

There are notes on character levels.
This table provides a mapping from FW level to Dice/CP.

    +-------+---------------------------------+-------+----------------------+-----------+
    | Level | Title                           | Incr. | Budget Dice          | Budget CP |
    +=======+=================================+=======+======================+===========+
    | 1     | Priest or Mage                  | +0D   | 18D attr, 7D skills  | 25*3 = 75 |
    +-------+---------------------------------+-------+----------------------+-----------+
    | 2-3   | High Priest or High Mage        | +4D   | 20D attr, 9D skills  | 29*3 = 87 |
    +-------+---------------------------------+-------+----------------------+-----------+
    | 4-5   | Senior Priest or Senior Mage    | +8D   | 22D attr, 11D skills | 33*3 = 99 |
    +-------+---------------------------------+-------+----------------------+-----------+

From this, we have budgetary guidelines for NPC design.
