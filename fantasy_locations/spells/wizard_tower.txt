
Basic Shelter
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

:Skill: Conjuration
:Difficulty: 17 
:Effect: 6 (shelter: 1*D; environmental_resistance (R1) +3D to stamina or Physique against heat, cold, or pressure)
:Range: 1 m \(0)
:Speed:  \(0)
:Duration: 10 hr \(23)
:Casting Time: 1 min \(9)

:Other Aspects:

    area_effect (12): 2m height 1.25m width 2m depth cuboid; fluid_shape


    components (2): pinch of brick, stone, wood, paper, or cloth (ordinary; destroyed)


    focused (6): Focus based on effect, duration


    gestures (2): slowly sprinkle pinch in circle around caster (simple)


    incantation (2): Give me shelter (sentence)


    other_alterant (2): features...





This spell is quite useful for the single, traveling
caster. This creates a cramped shelter around the caster.
The shelter has a door to exit it and a small fireplace
in one corner so a fire can be set to create warmth and
light. The thin walls, seemingly made of the same material 
as the component, give the caster some protection
from the elements and physical attacks. As the spell is
focused, the caster may leave the hut without worrying
about it disappearing.    



Improved Hut
~~~~~~~~~~~~

:Skill: Conjuration
:Difficulty: 26 
:Effect: 21 (shelter: 5*D; environmental_resistance (R2) +6D to stamina or Physique against heat, cold, or pressure)
:Range: 1 m \(0)
:Speed:  \(0)
:Duration: 1 day \(25)
:Casting Time: 5 min \(12)

:Other Aspects:

    area_effect (15): 1m height 4m width 4m depth cuboid; fluid_shape


    components (4): painted toy house (uncommon)


    concentration (1): Concentration: 3.5 sec (willpower/mettle roll 7)


    focused (9): Focus based on effect, duration


    gestures (2): place toy house on the ground and pretend to shape a larger house around it (simple)


    incantation (2): Give me protection from the elements (sentence)


    other_alterant (2): features...





Designed to accommodate a small band of close
adventuring comrades, this improved version of
the basic hut spell creates a domicile with thin walls
apparently composed of the same mater ial as the toy
house. Inside is a small fireplace, and there are uncovered 
windows and door, allowing in light. The shelter
offers excellent protection against the weather and predators.



Small Tower
~~~~~~~~~~~

:Skill: Conjuration
:Difficulty: 31 
:Effect: 22 (shelter: 5*D+1; environmental_resistance (R2))
:Range: 1 m \(0)
:Speed:  \(0)
:Duration: 25 yr \(44)
:Casting Time: 1 wk \(29)

:Other Aspects:

    area_effect (27): 2.5m height 10.4m width 10.4m depth cuboid; fluid_shape


    components (6): Mud brick or stone block from an ancient temple (extremely_rare)


    concentration (3): Concentration: 1 min (willpower/mettle roll 9)


    focused (13): Focus based on effect, duration


    gestures (3): Make hand gestures as if building a tower (complex)


    incantation (6): Read or recite loudly prayers related to the religion of the ancient temple (litany; loud; foreign_tongue)


    other_alterant (3): features...





This spell creates a retreat of any shape that the
caster desires, including whatever doors, windows or
stairs that she wants to include. It appears to made of
an ancient building material, and it provides terrific
protection for up to 25 years. The caster may change
the shape of the structure at any time during that
period. Anything inside will automatically shift without
breaking, to accommodate the new shape.



Keep in the Air
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

:Skill: Conjuration
:Difficulty: 29 
:Effect: 18 (shelter: 4*D; environmental_resistance (R2))
:Range: 1 m \(0)
:Speed:  \(0)
:Duration: 15 yr \(43)
:Casting Time: 10 hr \(23)

:Other Aspects:

    area_effect (27): 2.5m height 10.4m width 10.4m depth cuboid; fluid_shape


    components (15): Empty glass jar with fitted lid (common); white feathers (very_common; destroyed); gold dust (uncommon; destroyed)


    concentration (5): Concentration: 15 min (willpower/mettle roll 11)


    focused (12): Focus based on effect, duration


    gestures (1): Shake jar with feathers, gold dust, and air (very_simple)


    incantation (1): Blow air into jar (word)


    other_alterant (3): features...





After less than half a day of preparation, 15 minutes
of concentration, and a lot of shaking of some feathers,
gold, and air, the caster creates a movable tower. The
tower may go up and down or across the landscape at
a rate of three meters per round. It appears to made of
white marble flecked with gold and has as many doors,
windows, and staircases as the caster desires. He may also
change its shape at any point during its existence, with
the items quietly moving themselves around without
damaging themselves. At the end of the duration, the
feathers and gold dust turn to a fine ash.
